### Using the Bookmarklet

1. Drag the "Bookmarklet" button to your bookmarks bar.
2. When you find a page you want to bookmark, click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar.
3. You will be redirected to the app's form page with the URL, title, and favicon pre-filled.
4. Review or modify the details and click "Add Bookmark" to save it.
